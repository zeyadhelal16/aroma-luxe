### Common Questions
**Q: How do I change product prices?**
A: Edit the `price` field in products array in `/app/page.tsx`

**Q: How do I change colors?**
A: Find `amber-600` in components and change to other colors like `blue-600`, `red-600`, etc.

**Q: How do I add a new product?**
